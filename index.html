About Us

Sabotender Squad is a small, tight-knit community on the Behemoth server.

Our Focus

Our Free Company is looking to bring together FFXIV players both new and old to run a variety of content such as leveling dungeons, endgame raids, crafting, gathering and much more!

Perks

  • Various Free Company buffs, including increased EXP earned and reduced teleportation fees.
  • Large Free Company Mansion.
  • Access to fully customizable private chambers.
  • Access to in-house crafting facilities. (Increases max CP for level 70 and below)
  • A group Discord server for socializing and planning events.
  • Several veteran members willing to help players learn the ropes of FFXIV.

Contact Us
